Generative Research 2024

Understanding Financial Anxiety in First-Time Investors

TechCorp · Investing Feature Team

Led a 12-week generative study to understand the emotional and cognitive barriers preventing young adults from starting to invest. Through 28 in-depth interviews and a two-week diary study, we uncovered four distinct mindsets around financial risk. Findings reframed the product's core value proposition and directly informed a redesigned onboarding experience that increased activation by 22%.

Evaluative Research 2023

Loan Application Usability Overhaul

FinTech Startup · Core Product

Identified critical drop-off points in a multi-step loan application through a combination of moderated usability testing and session recording analysis. Ran 3 rounds of iterative testing with 8 participants each, tracking task completion and error rates across redesign cycles. The final design reduced time-on-task by 41% and decreased abandonment at the income-verification step by 34%.

Moderated Usability Testing Session Recordings Task Analysis A/B Testing Iterative Design Critique
Strategic Research 2022

Healthcare Portal Accessibility & Trust Study

Design Agency · Healthcare Client

Conducted an accessibility-focused research programme for a regional healthcare provider's patient portal, with emphasis on older adult users and low-digital-literacy populations. Methods included contextual inquiry at community health centres, expert reviews against WCAG 2.1, and co-design sessions. Delivered a prioritised remediation roadmap adopted by the client's engineering team.

Contextual Inquiry Accessibility Audit Co-design Sessions Survey Expert Review
Research Ops 2023 – 2024

Building a Continuous Discovery Programme

TechCorp · Research Ops Initiative

Designed and operationalised a continuous discovery practice that delivered weekly user touchpoints across 6 product teams.
